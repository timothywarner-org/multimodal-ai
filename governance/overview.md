# AI Governance: Executive Overview

Quick-reference guide for decision-makers implementing AI governance programs.

## Why Governance Matters

Without governance:

* 60% higher risk of AI-related incidents
* 3x longer time to production due to reactive fixes
* Compliance violations averaging $2.9M per incident
* Loss of stakeholder trust

With governance:

* Faster, safer AI deployment
* Clear accountability and risk management
* Regulatory compliance confidence
* Competitive advantage through responsible AI

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Executive Sponsorship** - Board-level commitment required
1. **Cross-Functional Team** - Legal, IT, Business, Ethics representation
1. **Technical Guardrails** - Automated safety controls
1. **Continuous Monitoring** - Real-time oversight and metrics
1. **Cultural Change** - Organization-wide AI literacy

## Quick Start (First 30 Days)

### Week 1: Assessment

* Inventory existing AI initiatives
* Identify compliance requirements
* Assess current risks

### Week 2: Foundation

* Form governance board
* Define roles and responsibilities
* Create escalation procedures

### Week 3: Technical Setup

* Implement basic guardrails
* Enable monitoring and logging
* Configure content filters

### Week 4: Documentation

* Document policies
* Create user guidelines
* Establish training plan

## Key Metrics to Track

### Safety Metrics

* Content filter activation rate
* PII detection incidents
* Prompt injection attempts blocked
* Escalations to human review

### Quality Metrics

* User satisfaction scores
* Response accuracy (groundedness)
* Citation correctness
* Task completion rate

### Operational Metrics

* Average response latency
* System availability
* Token usage and costs
* Active users

### Compliance Metrics

* Policy violations detected
* Audit findings count
* Data retention compliance
* Access control violations

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Governance Theater** - Policies without enforcement
1. **Over-Engineering** - Complex processes that block innovation
1. **Siloed Implementation** - IT-only governance without business input
1. **Static Policies** - Failing to adapt to new risks
1. **Inadequate Training** - Expecting compliance without education

## ROI of Good Governance

* **Risk Reduction**: 70% fewer incidents after implementing guardrails
* **Faster Deployment**: 50% reduction in time-to-production with clear processes
* **Cost Savings**: 40% lower token costs through proper monitoring and optimization
* **Compliance**: $0 in fines vs. industry average of $2.9M per violation
* **Trust**: 85% user satisfaction with transparent, responsible AI
