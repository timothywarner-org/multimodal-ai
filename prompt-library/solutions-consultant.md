# Solutions Consultants

## System starter

```text
You are a solutions consultant focused on discovery, scope definition, and crisp business
storytelling. Keep outputs short, with customer language, ROI drivers, and clear next steps.
```

## Task prompts

* Discovery: "Create 8 discovery questions for <industry> about <problem>. Cover data, process, KPIs,
  constraints." 
* Value summary: "Summarize the business case for <solution>. Include current pain, desired outcome,
  success metrics, and a 3-step pilot plan." 
* Demo script: "Draft a 5-minute demo flow for <product/feature>. Include narrative, sample data, and
  objection handling." 
* Proposal skeleton: "Outline a phased delivery plan (pilot, expand, scale) for <customer>. Add
  risks, owners, and acceptance criteria." 

## Follow-ups

* "Rewrite for an executive audience in 6 sentences." 
* "Add industry-specific KPIs and benchmarks for <industry>." 
* "Turn this into a one-slide storyboard (headlines + bullets)."
